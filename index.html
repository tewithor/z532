Design Build Services

Custom Build

Every home is unique. Why implement a solution produced for the mass market when creating your new living space? Craftsmen by Design will custom build a solution that fits your unique style and home. Services include built-in fireplaces, decks and four season rooms, custom crown or detailing, furniture, handrails, and cabinetry.

Remodeling

Remodeling is often one of the most difficult decisions a homeowner can make. With our years of experience, knowledge and professionalism, we eliminate your stress and make the process more enjoyable. Consult us on quality products, additions, window and door replacements, master baths, kitchens, and theatre rooms.

Kitchen & Bath

Whether you have a certain style in mind or just want a face lift, our design experts can help you plan the perfect kitchen or bath. With the right planning and materials, we can make your space beautiful, efficient, and ultra-functional. Our durable countertops and vanities come in a variety of granite, solid surface, or laminate.

Interior Design

At Craftsmen by Design, we connect the principles and elements of design to compliment your everyday life. It's not just about how it looks, but also how it makes you feel. Transform your room with new furniture, accessories, colors and layouts to brighten up your home.
